    • Education and Training:

    I am not a Substance Use Counselor, but I am certified to facilitate groups and teach the social skills for success in the CHOICES C.B.I.’s curriculum. I have been facilitating group sessions since 2010. My training stems for a substance use background, which led me to serve 2 separate prison terms in a California State Prison where I received further training and developed my skills as a Substance Use Disorder Treatment (SUDT), and Anger Management facilitator, I also served as a Peer Mentor and the clerk for the SUDT and Anger Management programs. I have just recently completed training and am now certified as a CBT Practitioner and am continuing my training to obtain higher credentials.
